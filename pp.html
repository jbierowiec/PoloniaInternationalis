Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Polonia Internationalis! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We want to assure you that Polonia Internationalis does not collect any personal information, including location data, from its users. Our application is designed to function solely based on the NYC geolocation you have created, and no data is stored or transmitted from the user's device.

Location Information

Polonia Internationalis may request access to your device's location solely for the purpose of providing you with navigation services within New York City. However, it's important to note that no location data is collected or stored by Polonia Internationalis. The location information is only used locally on your device to provide you with the desired functionality.

Data Storage

We do not store any user data on our servers. All information collected or generated by Polonia Internationalis is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Polonia Internationalis employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Polonia Internationalis does not integrate with or use any third-party services that collect or process user data. Your privacy is our priority, and we have deliberately avoided incorporating any external services that could compromise it.

Children's Privacy

Polonia Internationalis is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website or within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Polonia Internationalis, please contact us at Contact Us.


By using the Polonia Internationalis App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to maintaining the security and confidentiality of your information.


Thank you for choosing the Polonia Internationalis App.
